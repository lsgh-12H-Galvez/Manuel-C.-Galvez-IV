Ideal Weight Calculator

The Ideal Weight Calculator is an app used to determine what is the most ideal weight you should have based on your height and gender. Everyone's body works differently, that's why there are four different comparisons for your ideal weight. Here's how you use the calculator:



STEP 1: Type in your height in feet without indicating any remainder inches.

Ex. (Height is 5 foot and 6 inches)

Correct Input - 5

Incorrect Input - 5.5 or 5'6



STEP 2: Type in your excess inches, not your total inches.

Ex. (Height is 5 foot and 6 inches)

Correct Input - 6

Incorrect Input - 66



STEP 3: Choose your gender.



STEP 4: Press the "CALCULATE" button.



You can then compare the outputs on four different formulas.

The app runs on Ionic, a program that allows the use of simple tabs-based layout for apps that are going to primarily use a Tabbed UI. This exports to the Ionic CLI.
